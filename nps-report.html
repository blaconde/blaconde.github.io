Project Case

NPS Reporting

Power BI reporting focused on customer experience measurement, trend analysis, segmentation, and management insight based on NPS survey data.

This project is based on real business data, therefore a visual preview is not included due to confidentiality constraints.*

Power BI Power Query DAX Customer Experience

Project Overview

This type of report is designed to support customer experience analysis by presenting Net Promoter Score results in a clear and actionable way. The dashboard helps transform survey responses into insights that can be used by managers and business teams to monitor changes and identify areas that require attention.

The main goal is not only to display the score itself, but also to show the context behind it — trends over time, differences between customer groups, and patterns that may explain changes in customer sentiment.

What This Report Can Support

  • Tracking NPS trends over time
  • Comparing customer experience across segments and groups
  • Identifying changes in satisfaction levels
  • Supporting CX improvement initiatives with data
  • Providing managers with a quick and structured overview

Analytical Focus

Reports of this kind can be used to explore how customer experience evolves across different dimensions, such as service channels, customer categories, time periods, or selected operational factors.

A well-structured NPS dashboard helps users move from a high-level view to more detailed analysis, making it easier to notice trends, compare results, and focus on the areas with the greatest business impact.

My Approach

  • Designing clear and management-friendly report layouts
  • Presenting key metrics in a simple and readable format
  • Building reports that support both overview and drill-down analysis
  • Focusing on usability for non-technical business users
  • Turning survey data into a practical decision-support tool

Why It Matters

NPS reporting is valuable because it connects customer feedback with business action. A good dashboard makes experience data easier to understand, communicate, and use in everyday decision-making.

This kind of project demonstrates my ability to combine analytical thinking, business context, and report design in order to create tools that are both useful and easy to work with.
