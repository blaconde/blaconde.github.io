# Sample project – Power BI

## Dashboard: Health & Lifestyle

![Demo](/assets/pbi_health.gif)

Power BI dashboard analyzing relationships between:
- physical activity
- sleep
- stress
- hydration
- mood

### Key insights:
- lower sleep duration is associated with higher stress levels  
- training intensity impacts both stress and activity levels  
- hydration correlates with mood  

### Technologies:
- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Power Query
